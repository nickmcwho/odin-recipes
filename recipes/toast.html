Sad Toast

Description

A step by step guide on how to make yourself the saddest little slice of toast you've ever tasted.

Ingredients

  • Wholemeal bread
  • Margarine
  • One cup of Arsenic

Steps

  1. Place one slice of wholemeal bread into toaster
  2. Toast bread until it is severely burnt
  3. Spread margarine onto burnt toast
  4. Drizzle arsenic onto burnt margarine toast
  5. Enjoy!
